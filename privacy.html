Privacy Policy

At EmailRefiner, your privacy is our top priority. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not store your email drafts. All processing happens locally on your browser to ensure complete confidentiality.

2. Usage of Information

We only collect basic analytical data to improve our website experience. No personal identifiers are tracked without your consent.

3. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our platform. We never share your information with third parties.
