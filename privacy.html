Privacy Policy for Email Refiner

Last updated: June 19, 2026

At Email Refiner, accessible from your website link, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors.

1. Information We Collect

Our tool allows you to input raw email lists to be refined. We do not store, save, or share the email addresses you input. All processing happens locally in your browser or is cleared immediately after the refinement process.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data provided is used solely for the purpose of removing duplicates or formatting the email list as per your request. We do not keep logs of the emails you process.

3. Cookies

Like any other website, our site uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed.

4. Third-Party Advertisers

We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
